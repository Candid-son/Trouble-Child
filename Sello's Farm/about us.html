ABOUT US

Discover Us and our Mission

Sello's GreenFoods farm is a company that produces and sells agricultural products(vegetables and fruits)
and promotes agrotourism. By specializing in selling both agricultural products and tourism experiences,
we present Agrotourism, which allows visitors to explore and engage with nature, eat from nature,
and understand the importance of protecting nature. we and are based in Maseru district.

Our mission is to use farming as a tool to fight global warming and over consumption of resources by promoting
sustainable development while improving the living standard of local poeple.We provide explicit farm-life experience
to both domestic and international tourists while also enchancing the quality
of life of local poeple.
